Pasta w/ ketchup

This recipe came to me during a moment of insipiration...

*one life story later

You'll need the following ingredients:

  • Pasta
  • Ketchup
  • Water
  • Salt

Steps:

  1. Fill a pot with water
  2. Add salt & heat untill boiling
  3. Add pasta & boil according to the packet
  4. Once done, drain the pasta of water with a strainer
  5. Add ketchup
